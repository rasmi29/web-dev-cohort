Media Format Guide

Format Type Description
MP4 Video Most widely supported format for video content. Can play on almost all devices.
WEBM Video A modern, open video format that is compatible with Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.
AVI Video A widely used format for video content, but is no longer recommended for its larger size.
MKV Video A widely supported format for video content, including support for audio and subtitles.
MP3 Audio An audio format, commonly used for music and sound effects.
OGG Audio An open format for audio files, with support for various codecs, including Vorbis.
WAV Audio An uncompressed audio format with high quality sounds, commonly used for sound effects and voice recordings.
FLAC Audio A lossy audio format, commonly used for music and sound effects.
